## 1. Make Sure You Include the Agent-Specific Actions in Your Policy


(Adjust ‚ÄúResource‚Äù to a more specific ARN if you prefer, but often you must specify `*` if you‚Äôre not sure of the agent‚Äôs ARN.)

- **Check** the ‚ÄúAgent details‚Äù in the Bedrock console and see which IAM role is associated.  
- Make sure that role also has the correct policy.  
- Alternatively, if you‚Äôre calling the agent from your code, ensure your code‚Äôs principal (IAM user / role) includes ‚Äúbedrock:InvokeAgent‚Äù permission.

## 3. Watch Out for Region or Account Mismatches

Sometimes you have the right policy but for a different region or account. For example, if your agent is in `us-east-1`, your policy or ARNs must reference `us-east-1`, not another region.
